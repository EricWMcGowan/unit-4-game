Crystal Collector

How To Play

  1. You will be given a random number to reach at the beginning of the game. Which is labeled "Number to Get
  2. Upon clicking on each crystal a different random number will be generated.
  3. You must reach the exact number to get in order to win.
  4. If you go over you lose.
  5. Have fun!
